WHY HOLBERTON SCHOOL?

  • Project-based learning approach.
  • Emphasis on peer collaboration.
  • Full-stack software engineering curriculum.
  • Income-share agreement (ISA) model for tuition.
  • Industry-relevant skills and curriculum.
  • Commitment to diversity and inclusion.
  • Global presence with multiple campuses.

Holberton School distinguishes out for its project-based learning, which gives students hands-on experience through real-world projects. The full-stack software engineering program includes both front-end and back-end development, delivering a complete skill set. The unique income-share agreement (ISA) concept enables students to repay tuition only after obtaining a job in the technology industry. The program is developed in partnership with industry leaders to ensure that graduates have the necessary skills for a variety of technical professions. Holberton School is devoted to diversity and inclusion, promoting a friendly and inclusive learning environment. Students gain from networking opportunities and exposure to different tech ecosystems thanks to campuses located throughout the world. The school's extensive career assistance services continue beyond graduation, assisting students with job placement and professional progression.
